Engineering

Built to disappear.

01

Private by architecture

Audio is captured, written, and discarded on your own machine. It cannot reach a server, because there is no server.

02

Fast on your own GPU

faster-whisper on CTranslate2 running large-v3-turbo. Roughly a second from spoken to written.

03

Works in every app

System-wide text injection places your words at the cursor anywhere you can type.

04

No system lag

A poll-only hotkey watches one key with no global keyboard hook, so it never fights your typing.

05

Always on, out of the way

Lives in the tray, restarts itself if it hiccups, warms the model at launch for an instant first word.

06

Free and open

No subscription, no account, no telemetry on your voice. The whole thing is yours to read and build.
