Three moves. That is the whole app.

  1. Right Altx2

    Double-tap to start

    The pill slides up and Svara starts listening. No window to find, no button to hunt for.

  2. Speak naturally

    Words stream in as you talk and get cleaned up on the fly. Raise your voice and Svara types it IN CAPS.

  3. Tap to finish

    Text lands right at your cursor, in whatever app you are using. Slack, VS Code, your browser, a terminal.

Built to disappear into your workflow.

Everything runs on your device. No account, no cloud, no telemetry reading your voice.

Private by architecture

Your audio is captured, transcribed, and discarded on your own machine. It physically cannot reach a server, because there is no server. Works on a plane, in a vault, offline.

GPU-fast streaming

Powered by faster-whisper running large-v3-turbo on CTranslate2. Words appear as you speak, roughly a second behind your voice.

Works in every app

System-wide text injection through the Win32 API drops text at your cursor anywhere you can type.

A UI you will actually enjoy

Twelve themes, eight sound visualizers, ten backgrounds. Drag it anywhere, dock it, or click for a little surprise. It dodges your cursor so it never sits on your text.

Always on, out of the way

Lives in the tray, restarts itself if it ever hiccups, and uses no global keyboard hook. It watches one key and nothing else.

Make it yours in a tap.

The pill up top is live. Try a look and watch it change, the same way it does on your desktop.

For the people who read the source.

stt

faster-whisper on CTranslate2, large-v3-turbo at int8, warmed up at launch for an instant first word.

vad

Silero voice activity detection with a pre-roll ring buffer, so the first syllable is never clipped.

input

Poll-only hotkey via GetAsyncKeyState. No system-wide keyboard hook, so it can never interfere with your typing.

output

Win32 SendInput and clipboard paste for character-perfect text injection into any focused app.

ui

Per-pixel alpha overlay rendered with Pillow and UpdateLayeredWindow. Smooth edges, real glow, no jagged pixels.

ship

Packaged with PyInstaller into a self-contained folder. Your users double-click one exe. No Python required.
