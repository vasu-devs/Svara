Engineering

Built to disappear.

01

Private by architecture

Your audio is captured, written, and discarded on your own machine. It cannot reach a server, because there is no server.

02

Fast on your own GPU

faster-whisper on CTranslate2, large-v3-turbo. Roughly a second from spoken to written.

03

Works in every app

System-wide text injection sets your words at the cursor anywhere you can type.

04

A pill you enjoy

Eight visualizers and a draggable overlay that dodges your cursor so it never sits on your text.

05

Always on, out of the way

Lives in the tray, restarts itself, watches one key with no keyboard hook.

Under the hood

For the people who read the source.

stt

faster-whisper on CTranslate2, large-v3-turbo at int8, warmed up at launch for an instant first word.

vad

Silero voice activity detection with a pre-roll buffer, so the first syllable is never clipped.

input

Poll-only hotkey via GetAsyncKeyState. No system-wide hook, so it never interferes with your typing.

output

Win32 SendInput and clipboard paste for character-perfect injection into any focused app.

ui

Per-pixel alpha overlay via Pillow and UpdateLayeredWindow. Smooth edges, no jagged pixels.

ship

Packaged with PyInstaller into one folder. Your users double-click an exe. No Python required.
